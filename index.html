About Bert

Reasons to choose

Bert was discovered during the time when there was a need for raw ideas. After many years of casual art, it was time to take the national stage to another level and create not only for selfish needs but for the world.Bert is a franchise that has established itself on the strong foundations of "Authenticity amidst Diversity"

Albert William, the founder and owner of Bert, has been working on many projects over the many years including the designs on Ndesag Designs and Youth Christ Believers.
